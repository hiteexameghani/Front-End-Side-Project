Our Story  

Imagine a woman unexpectedly going into labour at a midwives' office with her toddler by her side and her husband hours away....
lt was at that moment amongst many others that Lulu Mashonganyika Founder and CEO of LiiT Care Connect had her dream of a safe and convenient platform connecting parents with childcare service providers was reignited.
Lulu was born in Zimbabwe and currently resides in Airdrie,Alberta with her husband and two miracle boys! Together with her amazing team, LiiT Care Connect continues to grow as a nurturing digital village to help streamline parenting supports and resources.
Welcome to the LiiT Care Connect Family!
